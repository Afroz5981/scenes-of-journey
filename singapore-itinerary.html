Day 1: Arrival & Iconic City Views

  • 10:00 AM – Arrival at Changi Airport, immigration & luggage.
  • 11:30 AM – Get SIM card, EZ-Link for public transport.
  • 12:30 PM – Check-in at hotel, freshen up.
  • 03:00 PM – Singapore Flyer & Time Capsule.
  • 05:00 PM – Walk to Merlion Park for views.
  • 07:00 PM – Dinner at Lau Pa Sat or Makansutra Gluttons Bay.
  • 09:00 PM – Return to hotel, rest.

Day 2: Gardens by the Bay – A Full-Day Experience

  • 08:00 AM – Breakfast at hotel.
  • 09:30 AM – Arrive at Gardens by the Bay.
  • 10:00 AM – Visit Floral Fantasy.
  • 11:00 AM – SuperTree Observatory for skyline views.
  • 12:30 PM – Lunch at Gardens by the Bay.
  • 02:00 PM – Explore Cloud Forest & indoor waterfall.
  • 03:30 PM – Visit Flower Dome.
  • 06:30 PM – Dinner at Satay by the Bay.
  • 08:00 PM – Return to hotel.

Day 3: Cultural & Instagram Spots

  • 08:00 AM – Breakfast at hotel.
  • 10:00 AM – Visit Ice Cream Museum.
  • 12:00 PM – Lunch at Arab Street.
  • 01:30 PM – Explore Sultan Mosque & Arab Street.
  • 03:30 PM – Little India: Visit Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple.
  • 06:00 PM – Shopping at Mustafa Centre.
  • 08:00 PM – Return to hotel.

Day 4: Universal Studios Singapore

  • 08:30 AM – Breakfast at hotel.
  • 09:30 AM – Arrive at Universal Studios Singapore.
  • 10:00 AM – Enjoy rides (Transformers, Battlestar Galactica, Jurassic Park Rapids).
  • 01:00 PM – Lunch at themed restaurants inside the park.
  • 03:00 PM – Explore souvenir shops & photo spots.
  • 06:30 PM – Dinner at VivoCity Mall.
  • 08:00 PM – Return to hotel.

Day 5: Sentosa Island Adventures

  • 08:00 AM – Breakfast at hotel.
  • 09:00 AM – Take Sentosa Express.
  • 10:00 AM – Explore SEA Aquarium.
  • 12:30 PM – Lunch at Sentosa.
  • 02:00 PM – Try Skyline Luge.
  • 04:00 PM – Relax at Siloso Beach.
  • 07:30 PM – Watch Wings of Time night show.
  • 09:00 PM – Return to hotel.

Day 6: Shopping & Departure

  • 08:30 AM – Breakfast at hotel.
  • 10:00 AM – Visit Orchard Road for shopping.
  • 12:30 PM – Lunch at a local hawker centre.
  • 02:00 PM – Explore Bugis Street for souvenirs.
  • 04:00 PM – Head to Jewel Changi Airport.
  • 05:00 PM – Visit Rain Vortex & final shopping.
  • 07:00 PM – Flight check-in & departure.
