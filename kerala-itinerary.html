Day 1: Arrival & Kochi Exploration

  • 10:00 AM – Arrival at Cochin International Airport.
  • 11:30 AM – Check-in at hotel & freshen up.
  • 01:00 PM – Lunch at a local Kerala restaurant.
  • 03:00 PM – Visit Fort Kochi & Chinese Fishing Nets.
  • 05:00 PM – Explore Jew Town & Mattancherry Palace.
  • 07:30 PM – Dinner at a seaside restaurant.
  • 09:00 PM – Return to hotel, rest.

Day 2: Munnar – Tea Gardens & Hill Views

  • 07:00 AM – Breakfast & drive to Munnar.
  • 10:30 AM – Visit Tea Plantations & Tea Museum.
  • 01:00 PM – Lunch at a scenic café.
  • 03:00 PM – Explore Eravikulam National Park.
  • 05:30 PM – Visit Mattupetty Dam & Echo Point.
  • 08:00 PM – Dinner at a traditional Kerala restaurant.

Day 3: Thekkady – Wildlife & Cultural Shows

  • 07:00 AM – Breakfast & drive to Thekkady.
  • 10:00 AM – Check-in at hotel.
  • 11:30 AM – Boating at Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • 02:00 PM – Lunch at a spice garden restaurant.
  • 04:00 PM – Kathakali dance & Kalaripayattu show.
  • 07:30 PM – Dinner & return to hotel.

Day 4: Alleppey – Houseboat Experience

  • 07:30 AM – Breakfast & drive to Alleppey.
  • 10:00 AM – Check-in at houseboat.
  • 12:00 PM – Traditional Kerala lunch on the boat.
  • 03:00 PM – Cruise through backwaters & scenic villages.
  • 06:30 PM – Sunset viewing from houseboat.
  • 08:00 PM – Dinner & overnight stay on houseboat.

Day 5: Kovalam Beach & Relaxation

  • 08:00 AM – Breakfast & drive to Kovalam.
  • 11:30 AM – Check-in at beach resort.
  • 01:00 PM – Lunch with a sea view.
  • 03:00 PM – Relax at Kovalam Beach.
  • 06:00 PM – Sunset at Lighthouse Beach.
  • 08:00 PM – Dinner & return to resort.

Day 6: Trivandrum & Departure

  • 08:00 AM – Breakfast & drive to Trivandrum.
  • 10:00 AM – Visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple.
  • 12:00 PM – Explore Napier Museum & Zoo.
  • 02:00 PM – Lunch at a Kerala-themed restaurant.
  • 04:00 PM – Visit local markets for souvenirs.
  • 06:00 PM – Depart for Trivandrum Airport.
